Our Vision

The rapid pace of new therapy development and their approvals in hematology and oncology requires physicians to continuously consult clinical guidelines, prescribing information, trial registries, and the primary literature. AVOR combines official guidelines with the latest approvals and clinical studies, and structures them into a clear foundation for medical decision-making.

Developed by practicing hematology and oncology experts, AVOR puts clinical expertise at the center and supports it with structured evidence.
